What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that securely records and verifies transactions across multiple computers or nodes. Each transaction is grouped into a block and added to a chain of previous blocks, forming an immutable record. Blockchain ensures transparency, immutability, and security, making it an ideal platform for various applications.
